Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1 December 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you. By creating an account or using the No2Dowry mobile application ("App"), you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a safe, reliable, and personalized matchmaking experience.

A. Information You Provide

This includes information you choose to give while creating or using your account:

  • Name
  • Gender
  • Date of birth (18+ only)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City or location
  • Photos you upload (profile or gallery)
  • Hobbies, interests, preferences
  • Marital status and basic lifestyle details
  • Communication shared voluntarily with our support team
  • Information submitted while participating in contests, surveys, or promotions

If you choose to use paid features in the future, you may be asked to provide payment details through a secure payment provider. We do not store full payment information ourselves.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

Device Information

  • Device model, OS version, app version
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Crash logs and app performance data

Usage Information

  • Date/time of login
  • Features you use
  • Profiles you view
  • Interaction activity (likes, matches, messages)
  • Basic analytics data (via Firebase)

This helps us improve app features, keep your account secure, and detect misuse.

C. Information We Receive From Others

If you choose to sign in through a third-party login (e.g., Google or Apple), we may receive basic profile information such as your name or email address.

We do not access your social media activity or contacts.

Camera Permission Usage

No2Dowry requests access to your device camera only to allow you to take photos for your profile or to upload images voluntarily within the App.

  • The camera is accessed only when you explicitly choose to use it.
  • No photos or videos are captured in the background.
  • Images are uploaded only when you confirm the upload.
  • Camera images are not shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

You can deny or revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Show relevant matches
  • Personalize your experience
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain safety and prevent misuse
  • Detect fraud, impersonation, and suspicious activity
  • Comply with applicable platform or legal requirements

We do not use your data for purposes not mentioned here.

3. Profile Visibility

To provide the matchmaking service, certain information you choose to share—such as your name, age, gender, location, photos, and interests—may be visible to other users on the platform.

You control what you share through your profile settings.

4. Third-Party Services

No2Dowry uses trusted partners to operate securely and efficiently, including:

Google Firebase (Authentication, Database, Storage, Analytics)
Firebase Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We may use additional service providers for hosting, analytics, moderation, or communication. These partners follow strict confidentiality and data protection requirements.

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

5. Data Sharing

We may share information in these limited situations:

  • With service providers who support our App operations
  • With law enforcement when required by applicable law
  • To prevent harm, fraud, or violations of safety policies
  • With users as part of the matchmaking functionality (e.g., your public profile)

We do not share your data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information as long as your account is active.

When you request account deletion:

  • Most personal data is removed within a reasonable time
  • Limited records may be retained only where legally required (fraud prevention, dispute resolution, security logs)

We do not retain unnecessary personal information after account deletion.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure Firebase storage
  • Access controls
  • Regular internal reviews

While we take strong precautions, no system is completely immune from risks.

8. Age Restrictions

No2Dowry is strictly for individuals 18 years and above.

We do not knowingly collect or store data of minors.

Accounts suspected to belong to minors may be restricted or removed for safety.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your region, you may:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or edit your information
  • Delete your account
  • Manage permissions (camera, location, notifications)
  • Contact us for any privacy-related requests

You can make most changes directly through the App.

10. International Data Processing

Your information may be stored or processed in countries where our service providers operate (such as India or the United States).

We ensure that appropriate protection measures are applied to your data.

11. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The App may use:

  • Mobile identifiers
  • Firebase analytics signals
  • Device-level behavioural analytics

These tools help improve security, performance, and user experience.

You can control certain permissions (like location or notifications) through your device settings.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically.

The latest version will always be available within the App and on our website.

Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
